welcome to mpo. this is the first release version! this is meant to replace the existing "media" app at wackybox.org.
press SPACE to pause/play, ARROW KEYS to navigate queue, C to clear queue, scroll while hovering over the red "100" to change volume. 
mpo supports basically every audio format a modern browser does, with tracker music as well.
files can be dropped onto the queue, or uploaded with the button.
you know there's a lot to check out at wackybox.org!
